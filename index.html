Entry rules — all 4 must be true
Rule 1 — EMA alignment
20 EMA above 50 EMA = long bias. Below = short bias. Flat or tangled = no trade.
Rule 2 — Price location
Long: price above 20 EMA. Short: price below 20 EMA. Never trade against EMA direction.
Rule 3 — S/R confirmation
Long: price near support. Short: price near resistance. This is your entry trigger.
Rule 4 — Minimum 2:1 R:R
TP must be at least 2x the SL distance. Below 2:1 = skip it, no exceptions.
Risk management
Risk 4% per trade
On $400 = $16 max per trade. Never exceed this no matter what.
Stop loss placement
5 pips below support (longs) or above resistance (shorts). Never widen your stop.
Breakeven rule
Move stop to entry once price moves 1:1 in your favor. Risk-free trade from there.
Max 2 trades per day
Stop after 2 losses in one day. Quality over quantity always.
When NOT to trade
30 mins before/after NFP, CPI, BOE, Fed
EMAs flat or tangled
R:R below 2:1
Asian session (8pm - 2am EST)
After 2 losing trades in one day
